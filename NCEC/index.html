About Us

We have been edging carpets since 2001. No carpet is too large or too small, we have all types of edge available, with hundreds of colours to choose from.
Most of our work is carried out on a 48 hour turn round, with smaller jobs done while you wait.
Our high quality carpet edging costs from 65p/linear foot, and can be attached to any shape or size of carpet. Trade inquiries are most welcome. With our while you wait service your fitters will be able to do a fast turn round on stair sets for example, or we are able to cater for larger work on site with our portable machine.
Please give us a call to discuss your precise requirements.
